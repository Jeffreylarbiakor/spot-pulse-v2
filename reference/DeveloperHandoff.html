2 RAG semantics

Always colour plus a text label — never colour alone. Thresholds on the 0–100 score:

Green — healthy · score ≥ 75
Amber — needs attention · 55–74
Red — at risk · < 55
StateFillText
Green--green-50--green-700
Amber--amber-50--amber-700*
Red--danger-50--danger

* Derived --amber-700 #8A5208 added for WCAG-AA text on the amber-50 fill (amber-500 fails AA as small text).

11 Live per-month figures Data

The dashboard is computed, not hard-coded. The month pills (Apr/May/Jun) select an index into each Spot's trend[] array; all dashboard figures recompute from that month.

Sourcespot.trend[m] (null = not yet reporting)
Spots trackedcount of all Spots
Avg healthmean of reporting Spots' scores
Check-ins loggedreporting / total
RAG donutGreen/Amber/Red counts via §2 thresholds
Cluster barsper-cluster mean for month m
